about

Yujie is an animation director, illustrator and visual artist. Her interests lay in exploring human relations and visualising personal sentiments. Her films often involve her attentive investigations of various drawing materials and different surfaces to create intriguing textures that lead to captivating works of media art. Her films have been shown in different international film festivals such as Slamdance Film Festival, Ottawa International Animation Festival, Video Art Miden, Tricky Women/Tricky Realities Festival, CURRENTS 2024 Art & Technology Festival, and many more.
